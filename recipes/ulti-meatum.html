"The Best Burger in the World"

The Ulti-Meatum is the burger seen in The Best Burger in the World. It is heard that it only comes out every century. It is served from the Grill 'Em Up Truck by its head chef, Ajay Maldonaldo.

Steps

AIt is made by stuffing a cheeseburger inside a cheeseburger patty, using two deep-fried cheeseburgers as buns. Then the special Himalayan Ketchup is added.

According to Muscle Man, the Idaho Style Ulti-Meatum is where the burger is made the same way, but with a whole bag of chips in it as well.
